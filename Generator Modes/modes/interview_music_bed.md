# Interview — Music Bed
**Mode ID:** `interview_music_bed`  ·  **Family:** Interview / Talking-Head
**Derived from edits:** PARKER new

Sparse, slow interview held together by a continuous music bed. Few cuts, long held reframes — feels editorial and composed rather than chopped.

**Best for:** Brand/founder films, reflective interviews, documentary-leaning pieces.

## Canvas

1920x1080 · 16:9 · 29.97 fps

## Pacing

~2–8 cuts/min · avg clip 8–20s

Cut logic: **narrative_beats + music_phrasing**. Let shots breathe; change on musical phrase boundaries and B-roll opportunities.

## Reframing

Method: AE.ADBE Motion · scale 100–120%

slow drifts / gentle push-ins

## Effect stack

- `AE.ADBE Motion` — slow reframe drift

## Transitions

_Choose the transition that fits the moment; never default to a hard cut._

**Default:** soft dissolve on music phrase

Library: soft dissolve on music phrase, gentle film dissolve, reframe-covered cut

Dissolve to B-roll and back on phrase boundaries; avoid abrupt changes.

## Audio

- **dialogue:** primary, ducked over music
- **music:** continuous bed, leveled under VO
- **use_jl_cuts:** yes
- **note:** Music sidechain-ducks under speech; swells in B-roll gaps.

## Color

- **approach:** cinematic editorial grade
- **lumetri:** filmic contrast + tone

## Titles & graphics

- **lower_thirds:** minimal/elegant
- **captions:** optional

## Toggles

`broll_montage`, `music_swells`, `elegant_titles`, `captions`

## Generator rules

1. Lay continuous music bed; duck under dialogue.
1. Cut on musical phrases and to B-roll, not on every pause.
1. Use slow push-ins to keep static shots alive.
